## Shared Foundations

Autonomy theory established that living systems are constraint-closed organisations.

APS builds directly on this.

## Key Clarifications

APS introduces:

- distinction between definition and diagnosis  
- explicit treatment of scale  
- integration with evolutionary explanation  

## Not Rebranding

APS is not autonomy renamed.

It is a structural clarification and extension.

## Key Point

APS stands in continuity with autonomy theory but formalises its explanatory architecture and extends it across scale and evolution.
