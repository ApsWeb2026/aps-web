## Introduction

APS reframes the social sciences by asking:

**What must be true for social systems to persist?**

---

## Beyond Individuals and Structures

Social systems are not collections of entities but organised processes that maintain their own conditions of persistence.

[[box:aps-box-hierarchy-vs-scale]]

---

## Institutions, Norms, and Culture

Within the APS account of social systems:

- Norms regulate activity in real time  
- Institutions stabilise constraints  
- Culture extends persistence across time  

Together, these organise social continuity across scales.

---

## Conclusion

APS provides a unified explanatory grammar for social systems and supports extensions such as economics as the organisation of viability conditions.
