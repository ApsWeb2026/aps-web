The APS Triad represents the foundational structure of the Agency–Process–Scale framework.

The three elements are not independent axes, separate components, or hierarchical levels. They are co-constitutive dimensions of living organisation:

- **agency** names the activity through which living systems maintain viability  
- **process** names the dynamic organisation through which this activity unfolds  
- **scale** names the spatial and temporal extent across which organisation is sustained  

This diagram serves as a visual anchor for the framework’s core claim: biological systems must be understood through the inseparable relation of agency, process, and scale.
