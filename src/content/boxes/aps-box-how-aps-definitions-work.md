**APS Box — How APS Definitions Work**

APS definitions do not function independently. Each concept—such as agency, process, scale, organisation, and normativity—derives its meaning from its role within a unified explanatory system.

Because of this:
- definitions constrain one another  
- changes to one concept affect others  
- terms cannot be reinterpreted in isolation  

APS therefore operates as a constraint-closed conceptual system in which coherence depends on maintaining these relationships within a constraint-closed system.
