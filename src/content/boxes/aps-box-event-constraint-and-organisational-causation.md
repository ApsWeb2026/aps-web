**Event Causation**  
One state produces another.  
Central in physics and chemistry.

**Constraint Causation**  
Conditions shape what processes are possible.

**Organisational Causation (APS)**  
Viability-oriented modulation of constraints within constraint-closed organisation.

**In brief:**  
Physics explains events.  
Biology explains organised persistence.
