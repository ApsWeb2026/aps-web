APS organises biological knowledge through a coordinated system that integrates definitions, conceptual articles, and research directions into a unified explanatory framework.

At the foundation are **canonical glossary entries**, which define core terms with precision and consistency. These entries establish the conceptual stability required for cumulative understanding.

Building on this foundation, **articles** develop and apply these concepts across domains, clarifying relationships, resolving ambiguities, and extending explanatory scope.

**APS Boxes** provide focused clarifications, resolving common misunderstandings, highlighting key distinctions, and reinforcing correct interpretation of core ideas.

At a broader level, **research streams** organise ongoing inquiry, linking conceptual structure to empirical and theoretical development.

This organisation is not hierarchical in the traditional sense, but reflects a coordinated explanatory grammar in which each component contributes to a shared structure of understanding.

**Key Point.** APS organises knowledge through a coordinated system of definitions, explanations, and research directions, enabling conceptual clarity and cumulative integration.
